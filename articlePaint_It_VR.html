Asymetrical VR game

"Paint it VR" is an Unity3d asymmetrical party game in which two teams of several players compete. Every turn, each player equips the VR helmet and draws in the space around him to make the other players guess a word or an expression. They can see in real time what draws the player equipped with the helmet through the retransmission of the game environment from multiple angles.
