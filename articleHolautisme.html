Mixed reality learning

Hol'Autisme is a catalogue of applications in mixed reality and a monitoring platform to help children affected by issues on the autistic spectrum to improve their social capabilities. I worked on Pop Ballons in the 3D (using some existing asset), Unity Integration and Game Design field.

Tasks Overview

Game Design field:

  • Co-design meeting with a social neuroscience researcher to define the scope of the new iteration of the Game design document
  • New iterations of the Game Design Document of the application in order to add a social aspect through the gameplay
  • Flow screen
  • Drafting of the list of assets and follow-up

Graphics field:

  • Rig, skin & animation (3ds Max)
  • 3D Modeling & UV Unwrap (3ds Max)
  • Texturing (Substance Painter & Photoshop)
  • Fx
  • Integration (Unity3d)
  • Optimize performance with customize shaders and use other Unity features
