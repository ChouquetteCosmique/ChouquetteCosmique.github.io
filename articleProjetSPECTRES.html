3 schools project

"Projet SPECTRES" is a 1 player student game made with Unity3d and using virtual reality headset. An experience in virtual reality in which the player embodies a bedridden character with vision problems. Between flashbacks and dreams, he will have to relive his day to understand how he got there.

The game has been presented during the 2019 edition of the Festival International des Jeux in Cannes.

As this project brings together students from 3 separate and distant schools, it was necessary to ensure the compatibility of the project with all VR headsets. Part of the team worked remotely throughout the project.
