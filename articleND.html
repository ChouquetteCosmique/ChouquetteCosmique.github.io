A cultural and entertaining application

"Notre-Dame de Paris: interactive model" is a Unity3d mixed reality application in which you can observe the cathedral, explore it by targeting points of interest, and thus discover this iconic monument of French history. As the only full-time person on this project, I was able to work from the design to the publication of this application.

Tasks Overview

  • Game Design Document of the application
  • Writing of narrator's speeches and texts of the application in english and french.
  • Flow screen
  • List of signs and feedbacks

Graphics field:

  • Meeting with experts in medieval architecture and improvement of 3D models

  • Texturing (Substance Painter & Photoshop)
