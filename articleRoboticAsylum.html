VR Cooperation

"Robotic Asylum" is a 2 players cooperative investigation game using virtual reality headset. Each player have to search and find clues to solve the crime, who, how and why did the Dr.X get killed ? Robotic Asylum takes place in a futuristic lab that does research on robots and offer 9 differents scenarios.

Tasks Overview

GD tasks :

  • 9 Scenarios with different level of difficulty
  • Work on the immersion of the player
  • Noise element in the scenarios

Graphics tasks :

  • 3D Modeling & UV Unwrap (3ds Max)
  • Texturing (Substance Painter & Photoshop)
  • Integration (Unity3d)
