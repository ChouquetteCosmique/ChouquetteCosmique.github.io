Bushidon’t is a die-and-retry multiplayer 2.5D party-game in which four samurais filled with selfishness have to battle against each other to get stronger in order to have a chance to defeat an upcoming boss alone for glory.

Tasks Overview

Graphics field:

  • UV Unwrap of 3D Asset (3ds Max)
  • Texturing in the scope of a rendering "toon-shading" (Substance Painter)
  • Integration (Unity3d)
